# Birsa-Munda-Tribal-Leader-Uprisings
Birsa Munda was one of the most prominent tribal leaders in Indian history. He led a powerful tribal movement against British colonial rule and the exploitation of indigenous communities in the Chotanagpur region (present-day Jharkhand). Because of his contributions, he is often revered as "Dharti Aba" (Father of the Earth) by tribal communities.
